The Codex.

The laws that govern the evolution of Avox. Failure to comply leads to immediate termination of access.

  1. MUTUAL RESPECT: Avox all players and administrators at all times. Toxic behavior is not tolerated.
  2. INTEGRITY: Exploiting glitches or using third-party hacks is strictly prohibited. Play fair or don't play.
  3. RP FUNDAMENTALS: Adhere to core RP rules including Fear RP, NVL, and Meta Gaming. Stay in character.
  4. ANTI-CHAOS: Avoid disruptive behavior or random deathmatching (RDM/VDM). Every action must have an RP reason.
  5. COMMUNICATION: Primary language for communication is English/Arabic. Clear comms over VOIP is required.
  6. LOVALTY: Advertising other communities or servers is forbidden and results in an permanent ban.
